About VOX Equity

Research that moves from evidence to impact.

VOX Equity translates lived experience, academic research, and community priorities into actionable projects. As the think tank of Voices of Equity, VOX supports student researchers, faculty collaborators, and community partners working toward measurable equity outcomes.

Research priorities

Four lanes of impact

01

Health Equity Research

Mixed-methods projects studying access, outcomes, and patient experience across underserved communities.

02

Policy & Briefs

Concise, evidence-based reports that translate research findings for institutions, policymakers, and community leaders.

03

Community Partnerships

Collaborations with organizations, clinicians, and advocates to ensure research reflects real community needs.

04

Student Scholarship

Training students in literature review, data analysis, manuscript writing, and ethical community-centered research.
